We are Blue Tide Scuba! We are located in beautiful Key West Florida, and have been serving the local area for over 20 years! We are experienced divers with a passion for marine life and preserving the waters. We love our jobs, and we love to help as many people as possible experience the beauty of the oceans. We are so excited you are here and hope we get a chance to dive with you soon!

We offer tours varying in times and price. Whether you want to squeeze in a quick dive near the shoreline, or want more of an intense dive, we have a tour for you!

Check out our tours page to pick the tour that is right for you, and give us a call to book your tour!

We can't wait to dive with you!
